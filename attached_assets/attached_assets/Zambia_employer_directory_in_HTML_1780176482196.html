Zambia Employer Directory by Province and Town (research draft as of 2026-05-30)

Methodology Note

This draft prioritises official government sites, company sites, hospital sites, airport operator sites, and clearly attributable directories; absence from the list does not mean an employer does not exist.

Important corrections to the seed list

  • 1) Zambia Sugar / Nakambala belongs under Mazabuka, not Lusaka.
  • 2) Dangote Cement Zambia's plant is in Masaiti District (often marketed as Ndola/Masaiti), not Ndola city proper.
  • 3) Radio Icengelo is in Kitwe, not Lusaka.
  • 4) David Kaunda National STEM Secondary School is in Lusaka, not Kitwe.
  • 5) Kasama is served by Chambeshi Water Supply and Sanitation Company, not Luapula Water.
  • 6) "Misuku Lodge (Medical Services)" in Kabwe was not kept because it was not a reliably confirmed healthcare employer in the sources reviewed.
  • 7) Kitwe Central Hospital / Kitwe Teaching Hospital refers to the same major tertiary hospital; use the current teaching-hospital naming.
  • 8) Chambishi heavy-industry employers belong under Kalulushi/Chambishi area rather than Ndola town.

How profession matching was assigned

Possible professions are matched by sector and typical recruitment patterns. For example:

  • Mining employers: Mining Engineers, Metallurgical Engineers, Electrical Engineers, Mechanical Engineers, Geologists, Environmental Engineers, Accountants, HR, Procurement, IT, Nurses/Clinical Officers, Heavy Equipment Operators, Welders, Electricians.
  • Hospitals: Medical Doctors, Nurses, Midwives, Clinical Officers, Biomedical Scientists, Medical Laboratory Technicians, Pharmacists, Radiographers, Public Health Specialists, Accountants, HR, IT, Records Managers, Electricians and Plumbers.
  • Councils: Local Government Administrators, Urban Planners, Civil Engineers, Architects, Environmental Health Officers, GIS Specialists, Accountants, HR, Records Managers, Lawyers, Community Development Officers, Procurement Officers.
  • Utilities: Electrical/Mechanical/Civil/Environmental Engineers, IT, Accountants, Supply Chain and trades.
  • Universities/Schools: Teachers, Librarians, Guidance Counsellors, Psychologists, Records Managers, IT, Accountants, HR, maintenance trades.
  • Banks: Accountants, Auditors, Bankers, Economists, Data Scientists, IT Specialists, Cybersecurity Analysts, HR, Marketing, Risk, Legal.
